Common Questions

Everything you need to know about Aiva HR.

Is it easy to switch from Excel?

Yes, we provide bulk import tools to upload your employee data from spreadsheets instantly. Our team assists with the setup.

Can employees access their own data?

Yes, the Employee Self-Service portal allows staff to view payslips, update personal info, and request leave without bothering HR.

How secure is the personnel data?

We adhere to strict data privacy standards (GDPR compliant). Data is encrypted at rest and in transit, with role-based access controls for your team.
