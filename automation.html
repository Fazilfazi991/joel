Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about implementing business automation.

How long does it take to automate a process?

Simple workflows can be automated in a few days. Complex, enterprise-wide automations typically take 2-4 weeks to design, test, and deploy.

Will automation replace my employees?

No, automation augments your workforce. It handles the boring, repetitive tasks, freeing your employees to focus on creative, strategic, and high-value work.

What systems can Aiva connect to?

We connect with almost anything. From modern cloud apps (Salesforce, QuickBooks, Slack) to legacy on-premise databases and mainframes via API or UI automation.

How much does a custom automation solution cost?

Costs vary based on process complexity. We provide a detailed consultation to audit your workflows and propose a tailored solution with clear ROI projections.
