It depends on complexity. An MVP (Minimum Viable Product) usually takes 2-3 months, while complex enterprise platforms can take 6+ months.

Do I own the code?

Yes. Once payment is complete, you own 100% of the Intellectual Property (IP) and source code. No vendor lock-in.

What tech stack do you use?

We use modern, industry-standard technologies like React, Node.js, Python, Flutter, and AWS/Azure to ensure scalability and maintainability.
