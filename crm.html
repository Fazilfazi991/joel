Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Aiva CRM.

Is my data secure with Aiva CRM?

Absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit encryption, regular security audits, and are fully GDPR compliant. Your data is backed up daily and stored in secure data centers.

Can I migrate from my current CRM?

Yes! We offer free migration assistance from any CRM platform. Our team will help you import your contacts, deals, and historical data with zero downtime.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide 24/7 email and chat support, comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and weekly live training webinars. Enterprise plans include dedicated account managers.

Can Aiva CRM scale with my business?

Absolutely. Aiva CRM is built to grow with you, from solo entrepreneurs to enterprise teams of 1000+. Upgrade or downgrade your plan anytime as your needs change.

How much does a custom CRM solution cost?

Since every solution is tailored to your specific business needs, we don't have fixed pricing. We provide a detailed quote after understanding your requirements, ensuring you only pay for features you actually need.
