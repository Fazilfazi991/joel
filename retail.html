Common Questions

About our retail platform.

Does it work offline?

Yes, the POS continues to work even if the internet goes down. Data syncs automatically once the connection is restored.

What hardware do I need?

Aiva Retail works on standard iPads, Android tablets, and PC/Mac browsers. We are compatible with most major receipt printers and barcode scanners.

Can I transfer my existing products?

Yes, you can bulk upload products via CSV or import directly from your existing Shopify/WooCommerce store.
